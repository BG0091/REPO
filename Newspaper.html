Death Of 11 People In Bus Accident

Bishesh Gautam

Kathmandu, Nepal

Breaking News

Kathmandu, Nepal - On February 12, 2025, a tragic bus accident occurred near Kathmandu, resulting in the deaths of 11 individuals. The incident took place on the Sankhu-Nagarkot road section of Shankharapur Municipality-5. The bus, en route from Nagarkot, veered off the road at approximately 4:50 pm. Initial reports indicate that the bus was carrying students, though further details are awaited. Rescue operations are ongoing, with authorities working to identify the victims and determine the cause of the accident.

This incident highlights the ongoing challenges related to road safety in Nepal. Earlier, on January 2, 2022, a bus veered off the road in Palpa, resulting in seven fatalities and 16 injuries. Similarly, on October 11, 2019, a passenger bus en route from Bhotsipa of Indravati to Kathmandu veered off the road at Tirkhumod in Sindhupalchok, leading to 11 deaths and numerous injuries.

The Sankhu-Nagarkot road section, where the recent accident occurred, has been identified as a high-risk area due to its winding paths and variable weather conditions. Authorities have previously implemented measures to enhance safety, including road improvements and public awareness campaigns. However, incidents like this underscore the need for continuous efforts to improve road infrastructure and enforce traffic regulations.

In response to the recent accident, local authorities have initiated an investigation to determine the exact cause. Preliminary reports suggest that factors such as road conditions, vehicle maintenance, and driver fatigue may have contributed to the incident. The findings of this investigation are expected to inform future safety measures and prevent similar tragedies.

The community has expressed deep sorrow over the loss of lives in this accident. Local residents have come together to support the affected families, offering condolences and assistance during this difficult time. The incident serves as a somber reminder of the importance of road safety and the need for collective efforts to prevent such accidents in the future.

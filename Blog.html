2. Butter Chicken

Butter chicken, also known as "murgh makhani," is a creamy, indulgent dish from Indian cuisine. It features tender chicken pieces cooked in a rich, buttery tomato-based sauce with a blend of spices like garam masala, ginger, and garlic. The sauce is smooth and velvety, often enhanced with a touch of cream to balance the heat and spices. Popular worldwide, butter chicken is typically served with naan or steamed rice, making it a favorite comfort food for many. The dish is a perfect combination of savory, spicy, and slightly sweet flavors.

3. MoMo

Momo is a popular dumpling dish, originating from Tibet and Nepal, and widely enjoyed across the Himalayan region and parts of India. These dumplings are typically filled with minced meat, such as chicken, pork, or buffalo, or vegetables, and seasoned with a mix of spices, garlic, and ginger. The dough is then folded around the filling and steamed or fried to perfection. Served with a tangy dipping sauce, momo offers a delightful combination of soft, savory filling and tender dough, making it a beloved snack or appetizer enjoyed by many.
